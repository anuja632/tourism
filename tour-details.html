Munnar / Thekkady - 4 Nights 5 Days

Travelling with your partner and family means hoping for a travel destination that offers the perfect romantic ambience. Perhaps mountains, serenity, and some much-needed peace and quiet are what you’re craving, and in that case, Kerala is the ideal spot for you to explore. If you wish to travel to this state that is known as ‘God’s Own Country’, then check out our Munnar–Thekkady honeymoon tour package.

This 4 Nights and 5 Days package covers the best of Munnar and Thekkady. Explore misty hills, tea plantations, waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and cultural experiences that make your honeymoon a truly memorable one.

Tour Plan

Upon your arrival in Cochin, our tour representatives will greet you and take you to Munnar. Visit the Athirappilly Falls on the way. Check in to your hotel and relax before heading out to explore Munnar. Visit the Mattupetty Dam, a beautiful concrete gravity dam, and then the scenic Kundala Lake, surrounded by forests and hills. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

Wrapped in peace and tranquility, Munnar is famous for its lush tea plantations and misty hills. Visit Echo Point, the meeting point of the Mudrapuzha, Nallathanni, and Kundala ranges. Enjoy activities such as boating and birdwatching. Later, visit Eravikulam National Park, home to leopards, tigers, giant squirrels, and sloth bears. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay.

After breakfast, drive to Thekkady. On arrival, complete the check-in formalities and head out to visit the famous Periyar National Park. Known as the Periyar Tiger Reserve, it is home to tigers, elephants, leopards, lion-tailed macaques, and Indian bison. Enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake and soak in the wilderness. Overnight stay in Thekkady.

After breakfast, explore the Green Park Species Plantation and Periyar Honey Bee Farm. After lunch, visit Elephant Junction for an exciting interaction with elephants. In the evening, go shopping for local spices, tea, and handicrafts. Return to your hotel for an overnight stay in Thekkady.

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive back to Cochin. Depending on your flight or train schedule, you’ll be dropped off at the Cochin Airport or Railway Station for your onward journey, marking the end of your memorable Kerala honeymoon trip.
