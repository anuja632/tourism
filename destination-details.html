Bangkok & Phuket - 5 Nights 6 Days

Experience the perfect blend of vibrant city life and serene beaches with our Bangkok & Phuket tour package. Explore bustling markets, ancient temples, and lively nightlife in Bangkok, then unwind on the golden sands of Phuket's beaches.

This 5 Nights and 6 Days package covers the best of both worlds—cultural sightseeing, island hopping, water sports, and luxurious beach resorts, making it ideal for couples, friends, or families looking for a tropical escape.

Tour Plan

Arrive at Bangkok International Airport and meet our tour representative. Transfer to your hotel and check in. Spend the evening exploring nearby markets or enjoy a relaxing dinner cruise along the Chao Phraya River. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

After breakfast, embark on a full-day Bangkok city tour. Visit the Grand Palace, Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha), and Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn). Explore the bustling streets of Chinatown and shop at local markets. Return to the hotel for overnight stay.

After breakfast, transfer to the airport for your flight to Phuket. On arrival, check in to your hotel. Spend the rest of the day relaxing at the beach or exploring Patong’s vibrant nightlife. Overnight stay in Phuket.

Take a full-day excursion to the stunning Phi Phi Islands. Enjoy snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, visit Maya Bay, and relax on beautiful beaches. Return to Phuket in the evening and enjoy free time at leisure. Overnight stay in Phuket.

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely day exploring Phuket. Visit Big Buddha, Wat Chalong, and the famous viewpoints. Spend some time shopping or relaxing at Kata and Karon beaches. Return to your hotel for overnight stay.

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Phuket International Airport for your onward flight. Bid farewell to Thailand with wonderful memories of your Bangkok and Phuket tour.
